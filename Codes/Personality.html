Welcome to Javindah's Personality Page

My strongest trait and emotions, hobby and how it impacts my academic life

Trait

My strongest trait is my determination. In life, we learn more from our failures than from our successes. Success wouldn't be rewarding if we weren't able to fail. Having a strong determination has allowed me to continuously stand up each time I fail. The true meaning of failure is giving up.

Og Mandino once said Failure will never overtake me if my determination to succeed is strong enough.

Sadness

When I am sad, I tend to reflect on life and the decisions I have made. This allows me to start over with all the knowledge I have gained.

Hobby

My hobbies are reading, going to the gym, travelling, and watching anime.

Reading



Reading stimulates the brain, reduces stress, increases relaxation, increases knowledge, and fosters critical thinking.

Gym

The gym is therapeutic; it increases my self-confidence and reduces stress.

Watching Anime

Anime teaches that determination without consistency, a goal, and a plan is not effective.

My Idol: Kobe Bryant



Kobe Bryant "Once you know what failure feels like, determination chases success."

Kobe Bryant was a determined and consistent individual; he taught me that talent without hard work doesn't guarantee success, and failure is an opportunity to learn. He also trained while no one was watching.
